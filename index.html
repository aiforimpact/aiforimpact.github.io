Overview

Join our AI for Impact: Venture Studio class. This course focuses on opportunities in the broad sectors of digital health, climate change and mobility with data and AI centric technologies such as blockchain, Web3, and privacy-tech, which can enable or accelerate major positive impact throughout the world. Mentors consisting of CEOs, VCs and academics work with student teams throughout the course. Students develop business plans suitable for real world impact which are eligible for seed funding dependent on judges' scores from demo day during the final class.

Click on the Fall 2022 button above to join the Fall 2022 class, AI and Web3 for Impact: Venture Studio.
