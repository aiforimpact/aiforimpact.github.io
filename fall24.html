Fall '24

Overview

Join MAS.665 Foundations of AI Ventures: Venture Studio class. The rapid developments in AI can enable or accelerate major positive impact throughout the world. This semester’s course will be structured as a "Venture Studio" with mentors and engagement with Scientists, VCs, CEOs and Academics. The course will focus on opportunities in the broad sectors of digital health, sustainability and mobility with data and AI centric technologies such as identity, privacy-tech, foundation models, blockchain, VR/AR and Web3. Follow these steps to register for the course.


  • Step 0: Register for course : For MIT (Link), For Harvard (Link)
  • Step 1: Questionnaire (Link 1) before September 5th
  • Step 2: Finishing the Online AI blockchain course (Link 2) by September 12th


You must finish Step 1 to be eligible to attend the first class. You will be expected to finish Step 2 and upload the completion snapshot before the second class. Note: if you have finished a degree course or have a professional background in these areas, you must still submit a form, but may petition for a waiver.

Course Outline

Class Goals:

  • Venture Studio format for-credit course where instructors seed and stimulate high-impact, high-value projects that are attractive for the best engineering, design, and builder students
  • Advance "Big Ideas" into MVP-seed-stage commercial consideration

Timeline:

  • Meet and Greet TBD
  • First day of class: September 5, 2024
  • Mid-term review: October 2024
  • Final Class Presentations and “Demo Day” awards: December 5, 2024

Class Structure:

  • Venture studio format, with team projects with 5-8 students per team
  • Guest speakers share leading-edge thinking
  • Minimum Viable Product (MVP) by the end of the term
  • Demo Day

Support for Students:

  • Weekly class mentor meetings on Thursday after class / guest lectures / work sessions
  • Class visits to local start-ups and technology companies
  • Data and AI scientists from startups for experience
  • Sessions with startup coaches

Funding:

  • Teams will receive milestone-based seed funds
